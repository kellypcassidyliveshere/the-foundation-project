Member Benefits & Learning Pathway

⚖️ Advocacy & Admin

  • Authorized contact / Referee
  • Complete complex forms
  • Witnessing documents
  • Help with Centrelink, ATO, & Medicare
  • NDIS Planning & Support
  • Bookings & Appointment management

💼 Employment Ready

  • Create professional resumes
  • Set up job alerts
  • Prepare for job interviews
  • Connect with employment services
  • Employment opportunities*

🌱 Wellbeing & Support

  • Welfare checks & Peer support
  • Establish healthy habits & routines
  • Link with Drug & Alcohol support
  • Locate mental health support
  • Design a fitness workout

🏠 Life Skills

  • Create a menu / budget
  • Educational resources
  • Sustainable living guides
  • Social & recreational event calendar

🚀 Pro Development

  • Business planning & Website design
  • Profile building & Professional emails
  • Mobile application development

The Learning Pathway Requirement

REQUIRED: WEEKLY CHECK-INS

Check-ins ensure progression towards employment within our community admin or external partners.

*Note: Professional Development and Employment services require completion of all learning programs. Active engagement in learning programs with weekly check-ins is required to Join the Board.
