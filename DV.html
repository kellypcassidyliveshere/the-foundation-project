Step 2
Centrelink Crisis Payment
⚠️ CRITICAL DEADLINE:
You must contact Centrelink within 7 days of your living arrangements changing.
  • Apply online via myGov (Payments & Claims > Claims > Crisis Payments > Crisis Payments)
  • Or call your regular payment line to register your "Intent to claim."
